32, The Record
32, The Record is a book of thirty-two photographs of a sign and mailbox that read "32" and "The Record," respectively. The photographs were taken between December 1, 2021 and January 1, 2022. The time and the date that the photograph was taken is printed beneath each photograph. Designed and printed in the spring of 2021 in Bennington, VT. The images are silkscreened, the type is 12 point Centaur, and the paper is Hahnemuhle Biblio. Edition of twenty-eight.
